Designing Spaces, Designing Stories: My Journey into Interior Design

From Digital Design to Physical Spaces

Coming from a background in digital design, I’ve always been captivated by how design influences the way we interact with the world. Digital experiences are carefully crafted to evoke emotions, guide actions, and enhance usability—principles that translate seamlessly into the physical world of interior design. Both digital and physical design require a balance of aesthetics and functionality, an understanding of the user (or inhabitant), and a thoughtful approach to storytelling through space.

When I transitioned from designing on screens to designing physical spaces, I realized that my foundational skills were highly applicable. Just as every visual element in a website serves a purpose, every piece of furniture, color choice, and lighting decision in an interior must contribute to the overall story of the space. I approach each interior project as I would a digital one—with a clear intention, careful attention to detail, and a desire to create an experience that resonates deeply with those who inhabit it.

The Power of Cultural Influence

Japan: Embracing Simplicity and Harmony

Having lived in Japan, my sense of design has been influenced by the country's unique approach to aesthetics—the appreciation for simplicity, natural materials, and harmonious balance. Japanese interiors are often characterized by a sense of minimalism, where every element has a purpose, and empty spaces are as important as filled ones. This philosophy has shaped my own approach to interior design, where I strive to create spaces that are not only beautiful but also purposeful and peaceful.

Nepal: Infusing Warmth and Richness

In contrast, my roots in Nepal bring a warmth and richness to my design style—a love for intricate details, vibrant colors, and the stories that every piece of decor can tell. Blending these cultural influences allows me to create interiors that are rich in character, where each space is a reflection of the people who use it. I believe that the best designs are those that celebrate individuality while embracing the cultural context in which they exist.

Designing for Well-Being

Interior design is deeply connected to well-being. The spaces we inhabit can have a profound impact on our mood, productivity, and overall quality of life. I’ve always been fascinated by how a well-designed space can uplift spirits, bring comfort, and foster creativity. From natural lighting that energizes a room to cozy textures that invite relaxation, I focus on creating environments that cater to both emotional and physical comfort.

One of my core principles is designing spaces that encourage connection—whether it’s a living room that draws people together for meaningful conversations or a workspace that sparks focus and creativity. By understanding the purpose of each space and the needs of its inhabitants, I create interiors that not only look stunning but also enhance the way people live and interact.

The Joy of Transformation

There is something magical about transforming a space—about taking an empty room and envisioning the life it will hold. The journey from concept to completion is one filled with creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration. I love working closely with clients to understand their visions, needs, and preferences, and then translating those into tangible designs that bring their dreams to life.

Every project is a new story waiting to be told—a blank canvas that holds endless possibilities. From choosing the perfect color palette to finding the right balance between modern elegance and personal warmth, interior design is about creating a space that feels like home, that tells a story unique to the people who live there.

Final Thoughts

My journey into interior design has been one of discovery and growth, a chance to apply my creative skills in a way that touches people’s lives in a very real, tangible way. Whether it’s a cozy apartment, a functional workspace, or a vibrant social space, my goal is always to create interiors that are more than just beautiful—spaces that are meaningful, inviting, and reflective of the people who use them.

If you’re looking to transform your space into something that tells your story, let’s connect. Together, we can create an environment that is not only aesthetically pleasing but also full of warmth, personality, and purpose—a space that truly feels like yours.
