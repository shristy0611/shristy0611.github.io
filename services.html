About Shristy

Shristy, an architect of digital narratives, blends engineering precision with creative artistry. With over a decade of experience spanning civil engineering and digital design, she crafts bespoke solutions that are visually captivating and technologically adept. Her work embodies a global sensibility, informed by her Nepalese heritage and international experiences in Japan. As a freelance designer since 2022, Shristy delivers diverse projects from UI/UX to web development, each reflecting her commitment to excellence and meaningful digital experiences. Her unique perspective allows her to create designs that are both globally appealing and contextually relevant, positioning her as a leading figure in the digital design landscape.
