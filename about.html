About Hi, I'm Shristy

| Creative Innovator | Culturally Diverse | Client-Centric

As a Creative Innovator in the ever-evolving landscape of digital design, I blend aesthetic excellence with functional precision, leveraging my engineering background to deliver innovative, impactful solutions. My journey from Nepal to Japan has enriched my perspective, making me Culturally Diverse and enabling me to create designs that resonate globally while respecting local nuances, enhancing inclusivity and relatability. Being Client-Centric, my practice is rooted in understanding clients' visions and needs. Through collaboration and active listening, I translate goals into exceptional design solutions that elevate strategic objectives.

How I Got Here

My journey from Nepal to Japan embodies a transformation from civil engineering to digital design mastery. Five years in infrastructure projects honed my analytical skills and precision. Driven by curiosity and passion, I relocated to Japan, immersing myself in cutting-edge design and software engineering. Since 2022, as a freelance digital designer, I've combined my engineering acumen with creative expertise. This unique blend allows me to deliver aesthetically pleasing and functionally robust solutions, reflecting my commitment to continuous learning and the synthesis of diverse disciplines for exceptional design outcomes.
